# Poly Haven scene sources

The coastal environment uses the following Poly Haven assets:

- Coastal Cliff 01 (`coastal-cliff/scene-2k.glb`) — https://polyhaven.com/a/coastal_cliff_01
- Pine Sapling Small (`pine-sapling/scene-2k.glb`) — https://polyhaven.com/a/pine_sapling_small

Poly Haven publishes these assets under CC0:
https://polyhaven.com/license

The online GLBs retain 2K source maps and full geometry, with WebP and Draco
transfer compression. Original external glTF inputs remain in the local source
archive.

The real-time water, procedural cloud sky, and day/night lighting are assembled
in Three.js. The continuous environment-preset approach was informed by
Christian Ortiz's MIT-licensed Stylized Components project:
https://github.com/cortiz2894/stylized-components
